Firozkhan Pathan - Founder

Nsquare Infra

Firozkhan Pathan

Firozkhan Pathan is a distinguished and highly respected figure in the construction and real estate industry, and the visionary founder of Nsquare Infra and Firozkhan Pathan. With over two decades of international and domestic experience, Firozkhan honed his skills at leading firms, laying a solid foundation for the magnificent projects he would later undertake. His professional journey began at Salunke Builders, where he laid the groundwork for his impressive career. He then transitioned to the international stage at Al Khateeb Interiors in Dubai, where he gained invaluable experience in the intricacies of high-end interior projects. He then returned to India to join Sun Estates Developers, a prominent name in luxury real estate, where he played a key role in developing opulent properties.
